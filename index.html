ABOUT ME

Hi everyone! My name is Danica Villez. I use she/her pronouns. I am a first-generation Filipinx student studying Computer Science at the University of Washington's Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering, also minoring in Diversity

Currently, I am serving as the President of the Filipino American Student Association at the University of Washington (FASA sa UW) , a 104 year old organization, home to nearly 140+ Filipinx-American students on the UW campus. I am also a Product Manager for RISE where I get the opportunity to build an app with a team of talented designers, developers and engineering managers from all over the country.

During my free time, you can find me hanging out with friends, drawing, or looking for new places to eat in Seattle.

MY "WHY"

I have found that I am passionate about three things:

1) Helping People
2) Social Impact
3) Problem Solving

My passion for helping people was ingrained in me at a young age by my parents. They taught me to always do what I can for others and to always have a smile on my face. My passion for social impact comes from my friends. They've shown me the power of a collective and constantly remind me that there is work to be done to dismantle oppressive systems. My passion for problem-solving comes from my mentors, who work to challenge me and tell me to never be satisfied without an answer.

I work to ensure that the the things that I involve myself with have to do with at least one or the other, but the tech field puts me at a unique intersection of all these three passions.
