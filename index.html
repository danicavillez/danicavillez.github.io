hello! i'm danica!

I use she/they pronouns. I am a queer first-generation Filipinx graduate of the University of Washington's Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering. At the University of Washington, I majored in Computer Science and minored in Diversity.

I am passionate about people, problem solving and social impact and have found that these passions intersect at technology. My goal is to bridge my experiences and interests and ultimately use technology to give back and protect the communities I was raised in. During my free time, you can find me playing my guitar, expanding my plant collection or going on fun walks!
